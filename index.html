Water on Mars!

NASA Confirms that Liquid Water Flows on Present Day Mars


In a new statement issued by NASA on September 28th, 2015, it is confirmed that: "New findings from NASA's Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter (MRO) provide the strongest evidence that liquid water flows intermittently on present-day Mars.". This is a major discovery as far as future explorations to Mars are concerned.
